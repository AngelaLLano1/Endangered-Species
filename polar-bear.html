Polar-Bear

The polar bear is a carnivorous bear whose native range lies largely within the Arctic Circle. It includes the Arctic Ocean and surrounding seas and surrounding land masses. The polar bear scientific name is Ursus maritimus. Conservation status: Vulnerable (Population decreasing). Speed: 25 mph (Maximum, Adult, Sprint) Mass: Male: 990 lbs(Adult, Beaufort Sea), Female: 330 to 550 lbs (Adult) Height: Female: 6.6 ft. (Adult, On hind legs), Male: 4.4 ft. (Adult, At Shoulder) Did you know: The scientific name of the Polar Bear is Ursus maritimus.
